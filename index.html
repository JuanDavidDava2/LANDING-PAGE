Feature Section

Request music in a club.

You can request, buy or vote for a song that you want put in the club.

Listen to the songs that are playing.

You have the possibility to see the tiredness that the dj can place and you can also hear them.

Get information about the clubs.

If you want to go to a club and you have not decided, you can check the app and see the type of music and the songs that are being placed in them.

Our inspiracion

The idea of ​​our application was born from the need for better communication in clubs, since generally in clubs, the interaction between the DJ and his audience is difficult, generally, if people want to ask the DJ for a song, they should write on paper or a physical list for them to read, making communication difficult. in addition to being used for the Portfolio Project for Holberton School.
